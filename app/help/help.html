Help

Hadoop Platform

In the Hadoop section of the portal you will be able to:
  • Access the user interface of different tools available in the Hadoop HDP platform
  • Monitor your jobs

PaaS Platform

In the PaaS section of the portal you will be able to:
  • See the catalog of available products
  • Launch a new cluster of a given product
  • See your active clusters
  • Query detailed information about your active clusters
  • Destroy any of your active clusters

SSH Keys

In the SSH Keys section of the portal you will be able to:
  • Check any registered SSH Key
  • Add new SSH Keys so they are allowed to access to your cluster
  • Remove current registered SSH Keys so they STOP having access to your cluster

Firewall

In the Firewall section of this portal you will be able to:
  • Check allowed IPs/Subnets
  • Enable or Disable IPs/Subnets, so they become active or not
  • Add new IPs/Subnets to your firewall
  • Remove those IPs/Subnets you no longer want
