Welcome to CESGA Big-Data Services Portal

You are into the Documentation section of this portal.

Here you can find information about how to use this portal.

Cloud Services Help

Into the Cloud Hadoop Clusters section of this portal you will be able to:
  • See your active clusters
  • Query detailed information about your active clusters
  • Destroy any of your active clusters
  • Launch a new cluster using a wizard

Bigdata Services Help

Into the Bigdata Services section of this portal you will be able to:
  • See your active services
  • Query detailed information about your active services
  • Destroy any of the services
  • Launch a new service using a wizard

SSH Keys

Into the SSH Keys section of this portal you will be able to:
  • Check any registered SSH Key
  • Add new SSH Keys so they are allowed to access to your cluster
  • Remove current registered SSH Keys so they STOP having access to your cluster

Firewall

Into the Firewall section of this portal you will be able to:
  • Check allowed IPs/Subnets
  • Enable or Disable IPs/Subnets, so they become active or not
  • Add new IPs/Subnets to your firewall
  • Remove those IPs/Subnets you no longer want
